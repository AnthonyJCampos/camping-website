Escape to Camp Sus

The annual camping trip since 2009. Join us for a fun and exciting outdoor adventure surrounded by nature.

From bocce ball to stargazing, there's an activity for everyone. Come make new friends, create lasting memories, and enjoy the simple pleasures of life. So grab your gear and join us this year for an unforgettable camping experience.

What to expect

4 Essential Elements That Make Camp Sus Unforgettable

Activities:

Get ready for non-stop adventure! With a variety of exciting activities, you'll never run out of things to do. Choose from classic games like Bocce Ball, Yard Pong, Toss, or try Hammerschlagen, a thrilling German game that's sure to get your heart racing. Cool off with a refreshing swim in the sparkling lake while enjoying stunning views of the natural beauty surrounding you.

Discover more fun →

Beverages:

Stay refreshed and energized with our selection of beverages. Choose from crisp water, thirst-quenching Gatorade, freshly brewed coffee, ice-cold beer, and more. With a variety of options to suit your taste buds, we've got you covered for your next outdoor adventure.

Dive into Our Beverage Selection →

Food:

Get ready to indulge in some seriously delicious!Our menu has something for everyone, from savory Pork Carnitas Tacos to juicy Brats with grilled onions and buns, and flavorful Chicken Stir-Fry and Chicken Fried Rice. Start your day with our Egg English Muffin Sandwiches filled with cheesy eggs and sweet Cajun Pulled Pork. Come hungry and leave satisfied!
