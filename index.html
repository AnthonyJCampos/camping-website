Escape to Camp Sus

The annual camping trip since 2009. Join us for a fun and exciting outdoor adventure surrounded by nature.

From bocce ball to stargazing, there's an activity for everyone. Come make new friends, create lasting memories, and enjoy the simple pleasures of life. So grab your gear and join us this year for an unforgettable camping experience.

4 essential elements that make Camp Sus the ultimate outdoor adventure.

Activities:

The fun never stops with a variety of activities to choose from. Enjoy classic games like Bocce Ball, Yard Pong, Ladder Ball, and Bean Bag Toss, or try your hand at Hammerschlagen, a traditional German game that's sure to impress. When you're ready to cool off, take a dip in the lake and soak up the beautiful scenery. With something for everyone, Camp Sus is the perfect for your next outdoor adventure.
