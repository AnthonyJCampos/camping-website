Rehydration Station

At our campsite, we've got all the water you need to keep hydrated and refreshed! We bring 65 gallons of H2O with us, since there's no nearby source of drinking water. Our trusty 55-gallon water drum and 10-gallon insulated beverage dispenser keep the water cool and accessible. And don't worry, when the dispenser starts to run low, we run the water through a Brita filter to make sure you get the freshest, tastiest water around! So, come on over and fill up your canteens - we've got plenty of cool, clear water for all!

Rise And Brew!

We take our coffee seriously! Every morning, our expert camp chef brews a fresh batch of rich and flavorful coffee in our large 24 cup coffee pot. Once it's brewed to perfection, we transfer it to our handy Lever Action insulated coffee dispenser, which keeps the coffee hot and delicious for all our early rising campers. And don't worry, we keep the dispenser full all morning long.
