Embrace Your Wild Side

Discover Your Next Thrilling Experience!

Our camp offers a wide range of activities to keep you entertained throughout your stay. Whether you're in the mood for some friendly competition or just want to kick back and relax, we've got you covered.

Things to Do

Unleash Your Competitive Spirit or Simply Kick Back

Swimming

Cool off from the heat and take a dip in the refreshing waters. Perfect for those hot summer days, swimming is a great way to relax and unwind.

Beer Pong

Test your aim and accuracy while enjoying a cold one with this classic party game. Perfect for a friendly competition with your camping buddies.

Bocce Ball

Roll your way to victory in this easy-to-learn, yet strategic game. Set in the scenic outdoors, it's a great way to enjoy some friendly competition and enjoy the beautiful surroundings.

Crokinole

This fast-paced and exciting game combines elements of shuffleboard and curling to create a truly unique experience. The perfect choice for a fun afternoon with family and friends.

Hammerschlagen

Show off your strength and precision with this traditional German game. With a few swings of the hammer, you'll be hooked on the thrill of the nail-biting competition.

Ladder Ball

A great way to get everyone involved, ladder ball is a simple and fun game that can be played by anyone. It's a great way to spend a lazy afternoon in the sun.

Movies Under The Stars

What's better than curling up under a blanket and watching a great movie? Doing it under a sky full of stars, of course! Enjoy some family-friendly movies in the great outdoors.

Paint Ball Target Competition

Put your sharpshooting skills to the test with this thrilling activity. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a first-timer, you're sure to have a blast trying to hit those targets.

Yard Pong

A supersized version of the classic party game, yard pong is a fun and easy way to get everyone involved. Great for groups of all ages, it's sure to provide hours of entertainment.

Walking

Take a leisurely stroll through the beautiful surroundings of the campsite. Whether you're looking to explore or just want to stretch your legs, walking is a great way to get some exercise and take in the scenery.
