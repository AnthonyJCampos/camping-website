Our Camp

Discover the Magic of Memory-Making at Camp Sus

Welcome to Camp Sus! My wife and I started this magical camping adventure back in 2009 with a simple idea - to create a space where friends and loved ones can escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and connect in a meaningful way. Every day is an opportunity to connect with others, push your limits, and indulge in the simple pleasures of life.

Our camping trips are more than just a chance to roast marshmallows and tell ghost stories (although we do plenty of that too!). We believe that every moment spent in nature is an opportunity for growth, both individually and as a group. Whether you're exploring the great outdoors or trying your hand at a new activity like Bocce Ball or Crokinole, each day at Camp Sus is filled with the potential for adventure.

And of course, no camping trip would be complete without some delicious food and drink to fuel the fun. That's why we take our culinary offerings seriously, always striving to deliver an unforgettable dining experience for our guests. From the coffee pot that brews fresh java each morning to the keg cooler that keeps our drinks perfectly chilled, we've thought of everything to ensure that our guests have everything they need to relax, unwind, and make memories that will last a lifetime.

How it Works

3 Simple Steps To Join Us On An Adventure

01

Get in touch:

You need to be invited by a current member or from the hosts themselves. Our Camp Sus is built on strong relationships and we want to make sure that everyone who joins is a good fit. That's why your friend will need to vouch for you and help us get to know you better before joining. Once we have completed our check, and you have been approved to join, you will receive an invitation to Facebook event where you will get access to all the details about the trip. Remember, each member is allowed to invite up to two new people each year, so make sure to ask your friend to invite you early!

02

Contribute:

Congratulations on being accepted to join us on our camping trip! You need to now contribute to the cost of the trip, which you can find under the pricing details section. This contribution covers the cost of all supplies, equipment, food, beverages, and more that will be used on the trip. The hosts will collect the contribution and ensure that all supplies are obtained. We believe that by pooling our resources it prevents duplicates and ensure that necessary items are obtained for the trip.

Please note: That the contribution does not go towards the hosts in any form of profit.

03

Pack:

After successfully contributing to the trip's cost, you're one step closer to joining us for an unforgettable adventure. The next step is simple - all you have to do is pack your tent, sleeping gear, and clothes, and don't forget a chair to relax in while you soak in the beauty of nature. And if you want to bring some of your own liquor, go right ahead! We've got you covered on everything else, from camping supplies to delicious meals and refreshing drinks. Just make sure to take the necessary time off work so you don't miss out on any of the fun.

Location

Where the Wild Things Are

Camp Sus is located in Wenatchee National Forest, a beautiful natural area that offers the perfect setting for a camping adventure. The exact location of the campsite is not listed on the website to preserve the privacy of this hidden gem, but rest assured that it is close to a serene lake, surrounded by breathtaking scenery and abundant wildlife. The location is ideal for those seeking to escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and immerse themselves in nature's tranquility.
